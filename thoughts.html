We're allowed to change from airplanes to swings.

A poem about facing the decision to change:

My perspectives are changing
of people
of buildings
of spaces
of air.
The deep roots of trees unfasten themselves
from the ground from which they came
Until my forest is empty
there's nothing left
but stillness
it's cold
and barren
and lonely
but there's nothing left to square my mind
my mind is free
free it is to sow new seeds
for the roots I spread now
will eventually blossom
and I will grow a new garden of my own design
that will beg a smile:)
